Fivnex - Our Branding

General Branding Guidelines and Rules

This web page needs further updating to be complete, however at this time we have all important information

General Brand Guidelines

  • Do not warp the logo
  • Do not alter or modify the logo
  • Do not add green to the logo or background
  • Do not recolor the logo
  • Do not rotate the logo
  • Do not use outdated versions of the logo
  • Text goes to the bottom and right sides of the logo ONLY

Spacing Guidelines

  • Padding should be NO LESS than 25% of the width of the logo
  • Text to the right should be 2/5th of the padding width
  • Text to the bottom should be 1/5th of the padding width
  • Text to the bottom should be centered vertically
  • Text to the right should be centered horizontally
  • The text height should be 3/5th or 4/5th of the logo's height

Color Guidelines

  • Make sure the logo is 100% visible
  • Use the outlined version for busy backgrounds with poor contrast
  • Best to put on dark colors
  • Do not put it on similarly colored backgrounds, unless using the outlined version
  • Do NOT put it on a green background under any circumstances

Colors:

  • #970079 (Powerful Purple)
  • #FF2846 (Respect Red)
  • #FFFFFF (White)
  • #000000 (Black)
  • #EB459E (Fuchsia)
  • #FE00EF (Magenta)

Icon Guidelines

  • Border-radius on HTML websites should be calculated in WHOLE NUMBER (or x.5) REM
  • Recommended radii on HTML is 1 or 2 REM
  • Icons with radii should either be rounded square or circle
  • Circle icons should be border-radius 100% on HTML

Font Guidelines:

  • Use Roboto or Roboto Mono
  • Do not use Serif fonts
  • Do not use Hand-written fonts
  • Fonts that help readers with dyslexia are allowed without question

Community Guidelines

  • Communities ALWAYS use the outlined version of the logo
  • Communities need to add any kind of SVG graphic to the logo
  • Disputes over customizations are NOT handeled by Fivnex
  • Logos that are similar to other Fivnex or Fivnex Community logos can exist

Merchandise Guidelines

  • Do NOT make Fivnex merchandise without contract
  • You must fill out the "Merchandise Contract" (either PDF, ODT, or EPUB)
  • Include ALL legal information required
  • You have to send 15% of income back to Fivnex
  • Send the contract to "legal@fivnex.co with the subject saying "Merchandise Contract"
  • Using any other Fivnex-copyrighted logos and properties (including but not limited to: Potabi, MEML, etc) also have to fall under these guidelines WITH 20% income return.
